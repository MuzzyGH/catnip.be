About Catnip

Catnip is designed to enhance your translation workflow without disrupting your existing tools and processes. Whether you use Trados, Smartcat, MemoQ, or any other CAT tool, Catnip integrates seamlessly to provide glossaries, revision tools, AI assistance, and machine translation on your terms.
